  • matchDurationToChildren()

    Ensures that the Duration of this DurationNode is appropriate considering the context of the Durations of its children.

    In many cases, this means that the Duration of this DurationNode is respelled according to the amount of beats that most closely matches the sum of the leveled and reduced children Durations (e.g. original DurationNode.duration = (2,16), and original DurationNode.relativeDurationsOfChildren.sum() = 13; new DurationNode.duration = (16,128)).

    In other cases, this means that the Durations of the children DurationNodes are respelled such that their sum matches the most-reduced form the Duration of this DurationNode (e.g. original DurationNode.duration = (7,32), and the amounts of beats for the children are [1,2,1]; the new amounts of beats for the children are [2,4,2], with a sum of 8).
